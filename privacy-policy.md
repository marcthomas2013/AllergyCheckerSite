# Privacy Policy for Allergen Checker

Effective date: 2 May 2026

Allergen Checker is designed to help you keep allergy information on your device and scan ingredient labels for possible matches. This privacy policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

## Information the App Stores

Allergen Checker may store the following information on your device:

- Allergy profiles you create, such as names for different people.
- Allergens, related ingredients, aliases, and notes that you save.
- Scan history, including saved scan images, recognised text, possible matches, and scan dates.
- App preferences, such as the selected allergy profile and whether you have acknowledged the safety notice.

This information is stored locally on your device using Apple-provided storage.

## Camera and Photo Library Access

Allergen Checker asks for camera access so you can take photos of ingredient labels.

Allergen Checker asks for photo library access so you can choose existing ingredient label photos to scan.

Photos are used only for the label scanning features you choose to use.

## Text Recognition and Matching

Allergen Checker uses on-device text recognition to read ingredient label photos. Recognised text is compared with the allergens, aliases, and related ingredients you have saved.

The app does not send label photos, recognised text, allergy profiles, allergens, notes, or scan history to a developer-operated server.

## Data Sharing

Allergen Checker does not sell your data.

Allergen Checker does not share your allergy information, scan images, recognised text, or scan history with third parties.

The app does not currently use developer-operated analytics, advertising tracking, or cloud OCR services.

## iCloud, Backups, and Device Services

Information stored by the app may be included in device backups or other Apple services depending on your iOS and iCloud settings. These services are controlled by Apple and your device settings.

## Children’s Privacy

Allergen Checker is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. Allergy profiles are stored locally on the device and should be managed by a parent, guardian, or responsible adult where appropriate.

## Deleting Your Data

You can delete saved allergens, allergy profiles, and scan history inside the app.

You can also remove app data by deleting Allergen Checker from your device. Depending on your device settings, some data may remain in backups controlled by iOS or iCloud.

## Safety Notice

Allergen Checker is a helpful aid, not a medical device and not a guarantee that results are complete or accurate. Ingredient labels, allergen advice, OCR results, translations, and product information can be incomplete, unclear, or change over time.

Always check product packaging, ingredient information, and allergen advice carefully before deciding whether a product is safe for you. If you have medical questions about allergies or intolerances, seek advice from a qualified healthcare professional.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
